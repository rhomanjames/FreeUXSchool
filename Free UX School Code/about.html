Hey, I'm John James. I'm a Senior UX Architect and Founder of Free UX School.

I created Free UX School to help give everyone a fair chance at breaking into UX...for free!

About me

I created Free UX School as a way to share knowledge I wish I had earlier in my career. UX bootcamps and college degrees are expensive, but that shouldn't keep people from learning UX. You're currently on the MVP of our site, so many improvements are coming soon!

Currently, I work with brands like Wall Street Journal, Barron's and MarketWatch as a Senior UX Architect . I specialize in UX strategy, architecture and UI design, so I created courses, articles, videos and a podcast on this site to help walk people through these topics for free!

I'm always open to new opportunities, feel free to contact me with details!
