You don't have to pay to learn UX.

We are an online school where you can learn about User Experience (UX) for free. We think it's pretty cool!

What’s the Catch?

No catch, no cap. We believe everyone should have access to high quality UX education in a format that works for them. Whether you’re a new grad, a career switcher or current UX professional, this school will provide you with key resources to break into and excel in the UX field.

You can will do it.

Learning about UX isn't the hard part, staying disciplined and focused is. I created a free workbook that can help you plan and stay focused on your UX journey. You will:
  • Address your mental barriers
  • Keep track of project ideas
  • Create a glossary important terms
  • Identify your dream jobs
  • More!
