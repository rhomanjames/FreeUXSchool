What is a Competitive Analysis?

By: John James

Approximate read time: 5 min

Overview

A competitive analysis is the process of evaluating and comparing the products or services of competitors in order to understand their strengths and weaknesses, and to identify opportunities and threats. In the UX research process, a competitive analysis can be a valuable tool for understanding the market and the user's needs and expectations, and for informing the design of a product or service.

Advantages to Competitive Analyses in UX Reseach

There are several advantages to conducting a competitive analysis early in the UX process:
  • It helps to identify opportunities and threats: By understanding the products and services of competitors, designers can identify opportunities to differentiate their product or service and meet the needs of the user in a unique way. They can also identify potential threats, such as new competitors or changes in the market, and take steps to mitigate them.
  • It helps to understand the user's needs and expectations: By analyzing the products and services of competitors, designers can gain insight into the features, functionality, and user experience that users expect and value. This can inform the design of the product or service and help to ensure that it meets the needs and expectations of the user.
  • It helps to inform the design process: By understanding the competitive landscape, designers can make informed decisions about the design of their product or service. They can identify best practices and trends in the market, and use this information to inform their design decisions.
  • It helps to identify areas for improvement: By comparing the product or service to competitors, designers can identify areas for improvement and prioritize their efforts accordingly. This can help to ensure that the product or service is competitive and meets the needs of the user.

Conclusion

Competitive Analyses are an amazing tool for understanding more about your competitors and the climate of the market your product or service is in. Try it out for your next project to ensure you are creating a product or service that truly stands out from the crowd.
