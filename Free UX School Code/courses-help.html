Help

How much do the courses cost?

$0. They're all free!

Can you grade my assignments?

At the moment, we don't have a big enough team to commit to grading every assignment. However, feel free to tag me on LinkedIn with some of your assignments. If I have the time, I'll leave some feedback!

Will you offer more in-depth courses?

We would like to, if time and resources allow.

Will these courses teach me everything I need to know?

These courses will teach you a lot about the fundamentals of UX research, architecture and design. However, we always encourage people to seek out other resources as well to diversify your learning experience. We don't know every single thing about User Experience, we're still learning! It's important that you continue to seek additional resources throughout your UX journey.

How can I contact you?

Connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email. I may not respond immediately, but I'll try to get back to you as soon as I can!
